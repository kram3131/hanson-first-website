The Medicare Gap

Medicare covers 80%. What about the other 20%?

Original Medicare (Parts A & B) covers a lot — but not everything. It typically pays 80% of your approved medical costs, leaving you responsible for the remaining 20% with no out-of-pocket maximum. That gap can add up fast.

That's where we come in. Emily and her team help you find the plan that covers your gap — whether that's a Medicare Advantage plan, a Supplement (Medigap) policy, or a Part D drug plan.

And our service to find it is always free.
