Common myths — busted

"Life insurance is too expensive." +
A healthy 35-year-old can often get a $500,000 20-year term policy for less than $30/month. Final expense policies for seniors often run $40–$80/month. Emily will find the most affordable option for your situation.
"I'm too old to get life insurance." +
You can typically get final expense and guaranteed issue policies well into your 80s. The premiums are higher as you age, but coverage is still available. Don't assume it's out of reach — let Emily find out.
"I have coverage through work — I'm fine." +
Employer life insurance typically covers only 1–2x your annual salary — often far less than what your family would need. And it usually doesn't follow you if you leave the company. An individual policy gives you portable, permanent protection.
