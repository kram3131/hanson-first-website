Founder Story

Emily solves the mystery for you.

Since she was young, Emily has been drawn to a good mystery. The kind that requires patience, a sharp eye for detail, and a bit of detective work to uncover the truth.

Today, she applies that same passion to the world of insurance. Insurance can feel like a maze of fine print and confusing deadlines, but to Emily it's just another mystery waiting to be solved. Whether you're trying to navigate Part D or wondering which Long Term Care plan fits your finances, Emily untangles the complexities to find the perfect fit.

She doesn't just find answers. She finds the right ones for you, ensuring you're protected and confident every step of the way. As a fully independent broker, Emily doesn't represent any single insurance company. She represents her clients. When she recommends a plan, it's because it's genuinely the best fit for that person's life, not because it pays the highest commission.

Based in Liberty Hill, TX, Emily has built her practice on long-term relationships. She doesn't just help you enroll. She reviews your coverage every year, answers your questions, and advocates for you when issues arise. Her clients know they can call her directly and get a real answer.

Emily is licensed in 37 states and works with clients across the country, while staying deeply connected to her local community through events, Medicare 101 workshops, and community health fairs.
