Adults 65+ Not Yet on Medicare

Bridge coverage while you delay Medicare enrollment.

Many adults 65 and older are not yet enrolled in Medicare because they or their spouse are still working and covered by qualifying employer insurance. This is completely legal and often makes financial sense — but it requires careful planning.

Hanson Insurance can help you:

✓
Understand your delay options

Confirm whether your employer plan qualifies you to delay Medicare without penalty.

✓
Find the right bridge coverage

Individual or supplemental plans that work alongside or instead of your employer plan until you're ready to enroll in Medicare.

✓
Plan your Medicare transition

When you're ready to move to Medicare, we coordinate both sides — the exit from your current plan and the Medicare enrollment — so nothing falls through the cracks.
