Program Description

Hanson Insurance Agency operates an SMS text messaging program to communicate with clients and prospective clients who have opted in to receive text messages. Messages may include appointment reminders, insurance updates, follow-up communications, and customer care notifications.

How to Opt In

You may opt in to receive SMS messages from Hanson Insurance Agency by checking the SMS consent checkbox on any of our web forms where a mobile phone number is collected. By opting in, you consent to receive text messages from us at the mobile number you provide.

Consent to receive text messages is never a condition of purchasing any insurance product or receiving our services.

Types of Messages

After opting in, you may receive the following types of text messages from Hanson Insurance Agency:

  • Appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Follow-up messages related to your insurance inquiry
  • Customer care and support messages
  • Important updates regarding your coverage or enrollment deadlines

The exact use-case for messages will be confirmed when you opt in. If you have questions about the types of messages you will receive, contact us before opting in.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. The number of messages you receive depends on your interactions with our office — for example, you may receive more messages during active enrollment periods or when you have an upcoming appointment.

Rates & Fees

Message and data rates may apply. Standard messaging rates charged by your mobile carrier may apply to messages you send and receive. Hanson Insurance Agency does not charge any additional fees for text messages.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of receiving text messages from Hanson Insurance Agency at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After sending STOP, you will receive a one-time confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.
